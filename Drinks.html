Lemon Mint Cooler

A refreshing drink perfect for hot days.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or honey
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

Mix: In a glass, mix lemon juice and sugar until dissolved.

Add Water: Pour in cold water and stir well.

Garnish: Add mint leaves and ice cubes before serving.

Iced Coffee

A quick and delicious caffeine boost.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brewed coffee (chilled)
  • 1/4 cup milk or almond milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Sugar or sweetener to taste

Instructions:

Brew: Brew coffee and let it cool.

Assemble: In a glass, add ice cubes, pour in the coffee and milk, and sweeten to taste.

Serve: Stir well and serve cold.

Berry Smoothie

A healthy and colorful drink made with fresh berries.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk or yogurt
  • Honey or maple syrup to taste

Instructions:

Blend: Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Serve: Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
