Chocolate Mug Cake

A quick and decadent dessert made in the microwave.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

Mix: In a microwave-safe mug, mix all dry ingredients, then stir in the wet ingredients until smooth.

Cook: Microwave on high for 1-1.5 minutes until the cake is set.

Serve: Let it cool slightly, then enjoy directly from the mug.

Fruit Parfait

A simple and healthy layered dessert with yogurt and fresh fruit.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain or flavored yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruits (e.g., berries, bananas, kiwis)

Instructions:

Layer: In a glass or bowl, layer yogurt, granola, and fruits, repeating until the glass is full.

Serve: Top with a sprinkle of granola or a drizzle of honey, if desired.

No-Bake Peanut Butter Cookies

Easy and chewy cookies that require no baking.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

Mix: In a bowl, mix peanut butter and honey until smooth. Stir in oats and chocolate chips.

Shape: Roll the mixture into small balls and flatten slightly.

Chill: Place in the refrigerator for 15-20 minutes to set.

Serve: Enjoy directly from the fridge or at room temperature.
