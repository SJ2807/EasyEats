Peanut Butter Banana Toast

A delicious and nutritious toast option for a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • Optional: Honey or chia seeds for topping

Instructions:

Toast Bread: Toast the bread slices until golden brown.

Spread Peanut Butter: Spread peanut butter evenly on each slice.

Add Banana: Top with banana slices and drizzle with honey or sprinkle chia seeds, if desired.

Serve: Serve immediately and enjoy.

Banana Smoothie

A refreshing and healthy drink you can make in minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

Blend: Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Serve: Pour into a glass and enjoy.

Microwave Scrambled Eggs

A quick and protein-packed breakfast option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Combine: In a microwave-safe bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.

Cook: Microwave on high for 1 minute. Stir and cook for another 30-60 seconds until set.

Serve: Fluff with a fork and serve warm.
